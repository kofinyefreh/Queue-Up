Queue up is your all-in-one task management tool — designed to keep your personal and work life structured. Create spaces, manage tasks (queues), track your progress, and never lose a thing — all powered by your browser's local storage.

Powerful Features

-- Turn on your productivity mode. --

Edit Spaces

Group your queues by themes or projects — whether it's “Personal”, “Work”, or “Fitness Goals”. Each space gives you a clean slate to stay focused on what matters.

Add & Manage Queues

Create new queues in seconds. Assign them to spaces, set details, and mark them when done. Keep your life flowing — one queue at a time.

Edit Anytime

Plans change? Edit queue details instantly without losing track of your progress.

View Pending Queues

Stay on top of what's due. All your pending queues are displayed clearly so you can focus your energy wisely.

Archived Queues

Keep your workspace clutter-free by archiving old or postponed tasks — they'll always be there when you need them back.

Completed Queues

Celebrate your wins! Access your completed queues to see how much you've achieved over time.

Local Storage Powered

No sign-ups, no servers, no stress — everything is securely stored in your browser. Your data, your privacy, your control.
